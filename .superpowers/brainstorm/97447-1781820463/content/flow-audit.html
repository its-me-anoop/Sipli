What's strong already

Editorial serif display type, mono micro-labels, a calm "paper" palette, just-in-time permissions, an answer-chip stack that recaps prior answers, and real water/wave motion on Welcome. The bones are good.

The gap

The branch is called tactile-vessel, but the vessel isn't tactile or continuous. It appears, disappears, and shows numbers that don't relate to each other. There's no single object carrying you from "Welcome" to "Done."
