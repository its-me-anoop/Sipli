Every sip tracked is one less plastic bottle. The Sipli Earth Day campaign runs April 20 – 26, 2026, with the main push on April 22. The core idea: a hydration habit built around a reusable bottle naturally skips single-use plastic. No invented stats, no greenwashing - just the honest refill story.

## An honest note

Sipli does not calculate "plastic bottles saved" or any similar eco metric. All campaign copy intentionally avoids specific numeric claims because the app tracks sips, not environmental impact. The message is about building the refill habit - the sustainability benefit is the natural byproduct.
