# Sipli — Smart Hydration Tracker

Sipli helps you build a healthy hydration habit with personalized goals, smart reminders, and beautiful progress tracking.

## Features

- **Personalized Goals** — Daily target calculated from your weight, activity level, workouts, and local weather.
- **18 Beverage Types** — Track water, coffee, tea, juice, smoothies, and more with realistic hydration factors.
- **Apple Health Integration** — Syncs workouts, calories, and water intake with Apple Health.
- **Smart Reminders** — Adaptive notifications that pause when you hit your goal and adjust to your schedule.
- **Insights & Streaks** — Charts, heatmaps, trends, and AI-powered coaching tips to keep you motivated.
- **Privacy First** — All data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

## Support

Having trouble with Sipli? We're here to help.

**Common Questions**

**How is my daily goal calculated?**
Your goal is based on your body weight and activity level. If you enable weather and workout adjustments, it increases on hot days or after exercise.

**Why isn't my goal adjusting for weather?**
Make sure Location permission is enabled in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Sipli.

**My HealthKit data isn't syncing.**
Go to Sipli Settings > Permissions > HealthKit and ensure it shows "Connected." You can also tap "Sync HealthKit Now" to force a manual sync. If it shows "Not connected," tap to re-authorize in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Sipli.

**How do I change my daily goal?**
Go to Settings > Daily Goal and enable "Custom goal" to set your own target.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Open iOS Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > Sipli > Cancel Subscription. You can also tap "Manage Subscription" in Sipli's Settings > About section.

**Can I delete an entry?**
Yes. Long-press any entry on the Dashboard or Diary to see the delete option. You can also tap an entry to edit it, then use the "Delete Entry" button.

**Does Sipli work on iPad?**
Yes. Sipli has a fully adaptive layout for iPad with multi-column views.
